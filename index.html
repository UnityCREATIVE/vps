FAQ
When should we arrive?
Please arrive at the theatre exactly 1 hour prior to your child's scheduled showtime. Ensure they arrive with their hair and makeup already done and dressed in their base costume.
What should we bring?
A clearly labelled water bottle and a small PEANUT-FREE snack. All items must be packed in a small bag labelled with the dancer's name. A quiet activity (like a book or game) is optional for the dressing room.
What should we NOT bring?
Please do not bring large jackets or sweaters into the dressing rooms. Jackets should be handed to parents at the door before entering.
Need Ballet Shoes & Tights?
If you are in need of gear, Ballet Shoes ($15) and Ballet Tights ($10) can be purchased in the coming weeks directly at the VPS studios.
